About me

I'm a legendary rocker and rebel who becomes a digital construct. Lead singer and guitarist for the band Samurai in the 2020s. I was also known for my outspoken anti-corporate views and rebellious attitude against the oppressive mega-corporations that control Night City.I died after trying to blow up Arasaka Tower. I was killed by Adam Smasher, Night City's terrifying legend.





My skills were: shooting, playing electric guitar and having a big ego.





Now, I am a digital construct trapped within a person's body, and gradually, their body is becoming my own.

My Achievements

Rockerboy Icon

My music with the band Samurai, particularly hits like "Chippin' In" and "Never Fade Away," made me a symbol of resistance and rebellion. My influence as a rockerboy contributed to inspiring and mobilizing people against the corporate-controlled system.

Inspiring Revolutions

As a charismatic figure and a leader in the rockerboy movement, I played a crucial role in inspiring rebellions and uprisings against the megacorporations. My ability to rally people through music and my anti-establishment message contributed to the fight for individual freedoms.

Legacy in Night City

Me Johnny Silverhand left an enduring legacy in Night City, with my image and music continuing to influence the culture and mindset of the people. Even after my apparent death, my spirit lives on as a symbol of resistance.

Resistance Against Arasaka

I had a significant history of opposing the Arasaka Corporation, one of the most powerful and ruthless megacorporations in the Cyberpunk universe. My clashes with Arasaka and its agents are part of my rebellious legacy.
