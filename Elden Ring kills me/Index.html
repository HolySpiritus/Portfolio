Game of the year winner

"Elden Ring" is a highly anticipated action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware in collaboration with acclaimed author George R.R. Martin. Set in a vast and intricately crafted world, the game promises a rich narrative experience intertwined with challenging gameplay mechanics. Players venture forth as the Tarnished, an exiled figure, on a quest to mend the shattered Elden Ring and restore order to the realm of the Lands Between. With its signature blend of atmospheric exploration, intense combat encounters, and deep lore, "Elden Ring" offers an immersive journey that pushes the boundaries of storytelling and gameplay in the action RPG genre.
